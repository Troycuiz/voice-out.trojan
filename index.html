About

PURPOSE

The essential purpose of this advocacy campaign is to bring awareness, influencing change of decisions as well as decision-making with regard to the rights of the LGBTQ+, prevention of bullying, tree planting, cyberbullying, and depression.

This movement stands out by dealing with problems, opposing unfairness, and building a society that is more fair for everyone. It is required to act to protect LGBTQ+ rights in order to attain equal treatment and acknowledgement for everyone; bullying prevention requires the establishment of remarkable environments that enclose and empower all the people;tree planting is needed for fighting against climate change and keeping the surroundings safe; tackling cyberbullying entails protecting people from online harm; and addressing the depression is necessary to promote the mental health awareness and support those in need. Advocacy is a key instrument in the creation of a better society, where more concern and kindness are apparent.

ABOUT US

Trojan is a company that advocates some issues in the community. The company's name is inspired by the trojan virus. The Trojan virus sneaks into systems with accuracy and effect, just as we sneak into societal measure and confront the circumstances with our bold initiatives. It was created to give voice to those people who are afraid to share their experience on unequal privilege. This advocacy is not only a mere concept; it is a potent catalyst for the positive change.
